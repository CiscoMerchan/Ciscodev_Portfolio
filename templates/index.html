Who I am

My name is Franciscoelias, but everyone calls me Cisco.

I entered the world of coding 2021 with a online Udemy course "Python Bootcamp". Since then,I have become familiar with programs such as Tkinter, Flask, Turtle, GitHub, Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, SQLite. I have created some projects through my course, as well as individual projects that I have displayed on GitHub. I have also joined a Meetup group called Python Beginners ,where I have done a presentation online (YouTube)of a step-by-step on how to create a log-in form using Flask and FLaskForm for beginners.

Coding is a fascinating domain for me, that I wish I found sooner. Being fluent in English, Spanish and French (and conversational in others), I can't help but draw similarities between coding and learning a language, something I really enjoy. There is always something new to learn, and constant problem-solving are other aspects of coding that really appeal to me #####(just to name a few.

My previous work experience as a Scuba Diving Instructor, and a manager in hospitality have required of me a high level of dedication and self-motivation, professional work etiquette, ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines and a high level of communication skill with people from all works of life.

In my spare time you can find me sailing, or scuba diving, reading about history, and of course with my family.

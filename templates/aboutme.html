About ME

My name is Franciscoelias, but everyone calls me Cisco. I entered the world of coding 2021 with a online Udemy course "Python Bootcamp". I have created some projects through my course, as well as individual projects that I have displayed on GitHub. In my spare time you can find me sailing, or scuba diving, reading about history, and of course with my family.
